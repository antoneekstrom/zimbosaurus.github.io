THE GOLDEN GOBLIN

!goldengoblin

The golden goblin is an elusive figure. Few people have ever witnessed its radiant skin and shiny tooth. It only appears when it wishes to, which happens to be very infrequently. The golden goblin is made of pure gold, this makes him a prime target for goblin-catchers. These people chase the creature across all of space and time. If one happens upon the golden goblin, they miiiight want to catch him. Before someone else does...
